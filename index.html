Air flight delay in USA Theguael

Dataset presentation

Over the last years, the amount of flights and U.S population have creased reciprocally. These raises had a direct impact on supply- demand pricing, enhancing the airlines offers. As matter of facts, were are more people who have the willingness to take a plane for short distance, than to travel in another means of transport like uses or personals vehicles. This fact came up with an increment the number of flight routes, population concentration areas and overcrowding airport runway.

Due, to the impact and cost any flight may cause, this work was conceived as an alternative of the many related proposals, particularly we think that enhancing the visual understanding by new visualization methods. As an example, an inexperienced person can understand the situation in order to make efficients decisions as the data comes up or simply provide an efficient tool to help experts analyze the air trafic.

This visualisation is quite more complex, it aims to helps users understand flights delays. This chart displays flight delays by companies over travel time and covered distance. The radius of the dots represents the average delay. Please keep in mind that this visualization is interactive, you can select a bubble to display several information about it. Those use may help any user who wants more information before chosing a flight ticket or even air flight companies in order to optimise their traffic and maybe, even use is as commercial plus to overcome the competition.
